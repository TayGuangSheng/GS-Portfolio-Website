About Me

Hey! I'm Guang Sheng, a Year 1 Computer Engineering student passionate about tech. I love unraveling the complexities of hardware and software, diving into digital logic, programming, and hardware design. Beyond academics, I'm an avid problem solver, tackling coding challenges, tinkering with Arduino, and exploring algorithms. During my free time, I spend my time volunteering, to give back to the community. Let's connect and explore the limitless potential of technology together!
